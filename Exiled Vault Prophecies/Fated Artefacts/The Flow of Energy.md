# The Flow of Energy
The Aesthete sustains herself in unnatural ways. When movement fails, energy flows.
## Prophecy
You will defeat Shavronne while holding Shavronne's Pace.
## Reward
Upgrades [[Shavronne's Pace]] to [[Shavronne's Gambit]] upon completion.
