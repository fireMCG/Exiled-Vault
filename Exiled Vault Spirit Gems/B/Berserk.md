## Berserk
Buff
Persistent
Tier: 14
Level: (1-20)
Reservation: 30 Spirit

Lose 0.1 % of maximum Life per Rage per second while not losing Rage
Grants (30-49) % increased Rage effect
Additional Effects From Quality:
