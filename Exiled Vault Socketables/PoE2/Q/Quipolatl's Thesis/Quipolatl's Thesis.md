# Quipolatl's Thesis

![[Exiled Vault Socketables/_Images/PoE2 - Quipolatl's Thesis.webp]]

## Details
- Limited to: 1 Ancient Augment
- Requires: Level 60

## Effects
- Helmets: A random Skill that requires Glory generates 15% of its maximum Glory when your Mark Activates
- Gloves: Each Runic Inscription from your Curse Skills causes you to Regenerate Mana per second equal to 10% of that Skill's Mana Cost
- Body Armours: +50% of Armour also applies to Chaos Damage while on full Energy Shield
