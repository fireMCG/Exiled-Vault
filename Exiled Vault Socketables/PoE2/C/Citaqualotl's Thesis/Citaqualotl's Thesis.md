# Details
- Limited to: 1 Ancient Augment
- Requires: Level 60

# Effects
- Body Armours: You Recoup 50% of Damage taken by your Offerings as Life
- Gloves: One of your Persistent Minions revives when an Offering expires
- Boots: Sacrifice 10% of maximum Life to gain that much Guard when you Dodge Roll
