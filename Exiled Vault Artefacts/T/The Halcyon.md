## The Halcyon Stats
- Requires Level 35
- +(20-30) to Dexterity
- Freezes you inflict spread to other Enemies within 1.5 metres
- +(35-40)% to Cold Resistance
- 30% increased Freeze Duration on Enemies
- 10% chance to Freeze
- 60% increased Damage if you've Frozen an Enemy Recently
