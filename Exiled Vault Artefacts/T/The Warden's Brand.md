## The Warden's Brand Stats
- Requires Level 30
- Adds 1 to 4 Physical Damage to Attacks
- Adds (5-15) to (25-50) Physical Damage to Attacks
- 30% reduced Attack Speed
- 15% chance to gain a Frenzy Charge when you Stun an Enemy
