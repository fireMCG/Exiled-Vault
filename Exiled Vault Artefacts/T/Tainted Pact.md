## Tainted Pact Stats
- Requires Level 42
- Regenerate (2-4) Life per second
- +(20-30) to Strength
- (2-3)% of Physical Attack Damage Leeched as Life
- (1-1.5)% of Physical Attack Damage Leeched as Mana
- Taking Chaos Damage over Time heals you instead while Leeching Life
