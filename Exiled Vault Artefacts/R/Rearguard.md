## Rearguard Stats
- Requires Level 33
- (20-30)% reduced Enemy Stun Threshold
- Adds (7–11) to (14–20) Physical Damage to Attacks
- +(150–200) to Armour
- (20–30)% increased Projectile Speed
- +(15–25)% to Block chance
- Can only be equipped if you are wielding a Bow.
