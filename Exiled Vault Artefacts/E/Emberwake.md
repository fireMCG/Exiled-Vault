## Emberwake Stats
- Requires Level 16
- +(20-30)% to Fire Resistance
- (30-40)% increased Fire Damage
- (5-10)% increased Cast Speed
- 10% chance to Ignite
- 40% less Burning Damage
- You can inflict an additional Ignite on each Enemy
