## Eye of Innocence Stats
- Requires Level 68
- +(16-24) to Strength and Dexterity
- 10% chance to Ignite
- (50-70)% increased Damage while Ignited
- Take 100 Fire Damage when you Ignite an Enemy
- 2% of Fire Damage Leeched as Life while Ignited
