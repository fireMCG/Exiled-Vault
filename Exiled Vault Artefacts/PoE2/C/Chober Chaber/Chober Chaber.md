# Chober Chaber

![[Exiled Vault Artefacts/_Images/PoE2 - Chober Chaber.png]]

## Description
The faithful may continue to serve, even after death.

## Details
- Two Hand Mace
- Physical Damage: (116-123) to (180-188)
- Critical Strike Chance: 5.00%
- Attacks per Second: 1.10
- Weapon Range: 1.3 metres
- Requires Level 33, 60 Str, 100 Int
- +100 Intelligence Requirement
- Adds (58-65) to (102-110) Physical Damage
- +(80-100) to maximum Mana
- +50 to Spirit
- Increases and Reductions to Minion Damage also affect you
