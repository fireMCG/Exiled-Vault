# The Flesh Poppet

![[Exiled Vault Artefacts/_Images/PoE2 - The Flesh Poppet.png]]

## Description
Long ago, the witches of the Azak Tribe learned
how to tap into the flow of Vivid lifeforce to
'cooperate' with their enemies... forcefully.

## Details
- Physical Damage: (81.7-94.6) to (134.9-156.2)
- Critical Strike Chance: 8.00%
- Attacks per Second: (1.34-1.41)
- Weapon Range: 1.2 metres
- Requires Level 40, 47 Str, 34 Int
- (90-120)% increased Physical Damage
- (7-13)% increased Attack Speed
- +(7-13) to all Attributes
- (40-30)% reduced Presence Area of Effect
- Copy a random Modifier from each enemy in your Presence when
- you Shapeshift to an Animal form
- Modifiers gained this way are lost after 30 seconds or when you next Shapeshift
