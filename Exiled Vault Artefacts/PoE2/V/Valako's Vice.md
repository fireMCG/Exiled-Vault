## Valako's Vice Stats
- Armour: 48
- Evasion: 44
- Requires Level 45, 40 Str, 40 Dex
- (50-100)% increased Armour and Evasion
- (5-10)% increased Attack Speed
- +(20-30) to Strength
- +(20-30) to Dexterity
- +(30-50)% to Lightning Resistance
- 100% of Fire damage Converted to Lightning damage
