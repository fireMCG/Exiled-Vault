# Flavour Text
In his broken madness, Xesht spills forth the hordes,
reaching out to wreak pain and ruination.

# Details
- Adds an Otherworldy Breach to a Map
- 5 uses remaining
- Breaches expand to at least 20 metres in radius
- Breaches remain open while there are alive Breach Monsters
- Breaches in Map have (-20-10)% increased Monster density
- Can be used in a personal Map Device to add modifiers to a Map.
