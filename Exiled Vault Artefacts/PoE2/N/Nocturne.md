## Nocturne Stats
- Buckler
- Chance to Block: 20%
- Evasion: 16
- Requires Level 5, 11 Dex
- Grants Skill: Parry

- +(10-15)% to all Elemental Resistances
- 20% increased Accuracy Rating
- Parried enemies take more Spell Damage instead of more Attack Damage
- 100% increased Parried Debuff Duration
