# Details
- Recovers (36-55) Mana over 2.00 Seconds
- Consumes 10 of 75 Charges on use
- Requires Level 49
- This Flask cannot be Used but applies its Effect constantly
- (80-70)% reduced Amount Recovered
- Right click to drink. Can only hold charges while in belt. Refill at Wells or by killing monsters.
