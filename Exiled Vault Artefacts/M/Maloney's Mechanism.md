## Maloney's Mechanism Stats
- Requires Level 45
- Has 1 Socket
- Has 2 Sockets
- Trigger a Socketed Bow Skill when you Attack with a Bow, with a 1 second Cooldown
- (7-12)% increased Attack Speed
- +(50-70) to maximum Life
- 5% chance to Blind Enemies on Hit with Attacks
