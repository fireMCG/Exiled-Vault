## Serpent's Egg Stats
- Requires Level 35
- (12-20) to increased Rarity of items found
- +(10-20) to all Attributes
- +(17-23)% to Chaos Resistance
- (20-30)% increased Mana Regeneration Rate
- Gain an additional Charge when you gain a Charge
