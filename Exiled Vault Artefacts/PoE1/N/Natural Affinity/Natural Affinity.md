# Natural Affinity

![[Exiled Vault Artefacts/_Images/PoE1 - Natural Affinity.png]]

## Description
You can take the girl out of the woods, but you can't take the woods out of the girl.

## Details
- Adds Nature's Patience
- Place into an allocated Small, Medium or Large Jewel Socket on the Passive Skill Tree. Added passives do not interact with jewel radiuses. Right click to remove from the Socket.
