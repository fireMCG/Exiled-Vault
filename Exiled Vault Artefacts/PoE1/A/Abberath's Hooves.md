## Abberath's Hooves Stats
- Evasion: (42-54)

- Triggers Level 7 Abberath's Fury when Equipped
- +(20-30) to Strength
- 15% increased Movement Speed
- (6-10)% chance to Ignite

- 1% increased Fire Damage per 20 Strength
- Burning Hoofprints
