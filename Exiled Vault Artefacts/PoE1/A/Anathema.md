## Anathema Stats
- Requires Level 49
- +(15-25) to maximum Energy Shield
- +(30-40) to Intelligence
- (10-15)% increased Cast Speed
- (10-20)% chance to gain a Power Charge when you Cast a Curse Spell
- Your Curse Limit is equal to your maximum Power Charges
