# Flavour Text
"In a time of great need, the tale-man sacrificed his very
body to bring forth the waters of victory and salvation."

# Details
- Requires Level 56
- +(20-30) to Intelligence
- Can't use Life Flasks
- +(55-70) to maximum Mana
- Increases and Reductions to Effect of Flasks applied to you
- also applies to Effect of Arcane Surge on you at (200-250)% of their value
- You have Arcane Surge during Effect of any Mana Flask
- Mana Flask Effects are not removed when Unreserved Mana is Filled
- Mana Flask Effects do not Queue
