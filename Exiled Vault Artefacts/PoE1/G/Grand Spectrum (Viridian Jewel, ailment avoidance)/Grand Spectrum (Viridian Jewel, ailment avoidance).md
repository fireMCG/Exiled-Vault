## Description
A mountain fortress safe from the storm.

## Details
- Limited to: 3
- 12% chance to Avoid Elemental Ailments per Grand Spectrum
- Place into an allocated Jewel Socket on the Passive Skill Tree. Right click to remove from the Socket.
