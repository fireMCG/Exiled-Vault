## Prospero's Protection Stats
- Requires Level 32
- Adds 1 to 4 Physical Damage to Attacks
- (4-6)% Chance to Block Attack Damage
- +(15-35) to Strength
- +(45-60) to maximum Life
- Armour from Equipped Shield is doubled
- Gain no Armour from Equipped Body Armour
