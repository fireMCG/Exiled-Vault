# The Front Line

![[Exiled Vault Artefacts/_Images/PoE1 - The Front Line.png]]

## Description
It was a great honour to fight at the front. The few that survived the initial charge were guaranteed fame and fortune.

## Details
- Adds Veteran's Awareness
- Place into an allocated Small, Medium or Large Jewel Socket on the Passive Skill Tree. Added passives do not interact with jewel radiuses. Right click to remove from the Socket.
