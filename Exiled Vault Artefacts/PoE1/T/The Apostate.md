## The Apostate Stats
- Movement Speed: -3%
- Requires Level 35
- Has no Attribute Requirements or Energy Shield (Hidden)
- +(30-50) to Strength
- +(20-30)% to all Elemental Resistances
- Gain Maximum Life instead of Maximum Energy Shield from Equipped Armour Items
