- Requires Level 64
- Duplicates up to 2 random Offer Rewards upon defeating the Herald of the Scourge
- Cannot have Boons
- This item is destroyed when applied to a Sanctum
- Unmodifiable
- Place this item on the Relic Altar at the start of each Sanctum run
