# The Vinktar Square

![[Exiled Vault Artefacts/_Images/PoE1 - The Vinktar Square.png]]

## Description
To the east,
it cannot be seen.
To the west,
it cannot be touched.
To the south,
it cannot be remembered.
And to the north,
it cannot be contained.
