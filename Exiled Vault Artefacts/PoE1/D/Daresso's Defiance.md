## Daresso's Defiance Stats
- Armour: (1316-1731)
- Evasion: (1044-1372)

- (180-220)% increased Armour and Evasion

- 2% of Attack Damage Leeched as Life

- You gain Onslaught for 5 seconds per Endurance Charge when Hit
- 100% increased Effect of Onslaught on you
