## Winterheart Stats
- Requires Level 42
- (12-20)% increased Rarity of Items found
- +(20-30) to Dexterity
- +(50-70) to maximum Life
- +75% to Cold Resistance
- Cannot be Chilled
- Regenerate 10% of Life per second while Frozen
