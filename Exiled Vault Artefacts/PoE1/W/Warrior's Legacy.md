## Warrior's Legacy Stats
- Requires Level 40
- +(20-30)% to Fire Resistance
- +(30-50) to Strength
- (20-25)% increased Melee Damage
- 30% chance to Avoid being Stunned
- 20% less Attack Speed
- Strike Skills also target the previous location they were used
