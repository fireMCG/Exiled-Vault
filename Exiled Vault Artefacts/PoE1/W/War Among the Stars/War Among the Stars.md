# War Among the Stars

![[Exiled Vault Artefacts/_Images/PoE1 - War Among the Stars.png]]

## Description
Conflict begets only suffering.
For some, that is by design.

## Details
- Area contains (4-7) additional packs of Elder Fiends
- Area contains (4-7) additional packs of Shaper Creations
- Socket this into a Citadel on your Atlas to increase the Tier of Maps and reveal hidden Maps in that Citadel's Region. You can only socket one Crimson, Viridian, Cobalt or Golden Watchstone into each Citadel.
