# Details

- +25% to Cold Resistance
- Minions have (10-15)% increased maximum Life
- Minions convert 50% of Physical Damage to Cold Damage
- Minions deal (25-35) to (50-65) additional Cold Damage
- Minions deal no Non-Cold Damage
