## Springleaf Stats
- Chance to Block: 22%
- Armour: (32-55)
- Energy Shield: (9-15)

- Requires Level 7
- +4% to all Elemental Resistances
- (80-120)% increased Armour and Energy Shield
- 80% reduced Freeze Duration on you
- Regenerate (30-50) Life per second
- Regenerate 100 Life per Second while on Low Life
