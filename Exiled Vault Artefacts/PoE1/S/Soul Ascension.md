## Soul Ascension Stats
- Evasion: (198-252)
- Energy Shield: (41-52)
- Requires Level 50, 39 Dex, 39 Int
- (120-150)% increased Evasion and Energy Shield
- +(17-29)% to Chaos Resistance

- +(-10-10) to maximum number of Eaten Souls
