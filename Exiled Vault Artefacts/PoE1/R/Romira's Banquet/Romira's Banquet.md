- (20-30)% increased Global Critical Strike Chance

- +(40-60) to maximum Mana
- 0.4% of Physical Attack Damage Leeched as Mana
- Gain a Power Charge on Non-Critical Strike
- Lose all Power Charges on Critical Strike
