## Details
- Requires Level 20
- +(20-30)% to Cold Resistance
- Adds (15-20) to (25-35) Cold Damage to Spells and Attacks
- +(200-300) to Evasion Rating
- Your Cold Damage cannot Freeze
- Immune to Chill
- Adds 60 to 80 Cold Damage against Chilled Enemies
