- Armour: 97
- Energy Shield: 35
- Requires Level 45, 46 Str, 46 Int

- (15-25) Life Regeneration per second
- (15-25)% increased Mana Regeneration Rate

- Take 30% less Damage from Hits
- Take 30% less Damage over time
