## Kingsguard Stats
- Armour: 208
- Movement Speed: -5.0%
- Requires Level 33, 68 Str
- +(60-80) to maximum Life
- +(40-60) to maximum Mana
- +(5-10)% to all Elemental Resistances
- 25% reduced Endurance Charge Duration
- Recover 5% of Life for each Endurance Charge consumed
