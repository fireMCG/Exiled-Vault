## Blood of Corruption Stats
- Requires Level 5
- +(20-30) to Strength
- Grants Level 10 Gluttony of Elements Skill
- Adds 19 to 43 Chaos Damage to Attacks
- -(10-5)% to all Elemental Resistances
- +(17-29)% to Chaos Resistance
- Corrupted
