## Bristleboar Stats
- Evasion: 30
- Movement Speed: -3.0%
- 50% reduced Evasion Rating
- +(40-60) to Maximum Life
- (3-5) Life Regeneration per second
- Gain 5 Rage when Hit by an Enemy
- Gain 10 Rage when Critically Hit by an Enemy
