## Blood Sacrifice Stats
- Lose 1% of Life on Kill
- Recover 1% of Mana on Kill
- Lose 1% of Energy Shield on Kill
- Corrupted
- Place into an allocated Jewel Socket on the Passive Skill Tree. Right click to remove from the Socket.
