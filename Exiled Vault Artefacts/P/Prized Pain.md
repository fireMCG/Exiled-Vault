## Prized Pain Stats
- Requires Level 48
- Adds 1 to 4 Physical Damage to Attacks
- +(20–30) to Strength
- Skills gain a Base Life Cost equal to 10% of Base Mana Cost
- Deal your Thorns Damage to Enemies you Stun with Melee Attacks
- (24–35) to (35–53) Physical Thorns damage
