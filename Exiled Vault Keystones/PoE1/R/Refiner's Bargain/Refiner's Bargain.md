# Refiner's Bargain

![[Exiled Vault Keystones/_Images/PoE1 - Refiner's Bargain.webp]]

## Description
No form is forged without forfeit.

## Effects
2% increased Maps found in your Maps
Equipment Items with Rarity dropped by Possessed Monsters in your Maps have 5% chance to be converted to Thaumaturgic Dust
Sulphite found in your Maps is granted as a random Ore instead at 15% of the value
Lifeforce found in your Maps is granted as a random Crop instead at 150% of the value
Unmodifiable
