# Amplified Energies

![[Exiled Vault Keystones/_Images/PoE1 - Amplified Energies.webp]]

## Effects
Imprisoned Monsters in your Maps have at least 1 Essence at the highest possible tier
(Essences in White Maps can reach a maximum tier of Screaming, and Essences in Yellow or Red Maps can reach a maximum tier of Shrieking)
