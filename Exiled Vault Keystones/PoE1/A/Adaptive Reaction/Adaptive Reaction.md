# Effects
Flammable Burrows in your Maps have 100% increased Explosion Radius
Hive Monsters in your Maps have 4% increased Toughness per Flammable Burrow destroyed
Hive Encounters in your Maps grant 2% increased Hiveblood
and Wombgifts per Flammable Burrow destroyed
(The higher toughness a monster has, the less damage it takes. A monster with 100% increased Toughness takes 50% less damage, a monster with 200% increased toughness takes 67% less damage, and so on. Reduced toughness causes monsters to take more damage)
