# Conquered Conquerors

![[Exiled Vault Keystones/_Images/PoE1 - Conquered Conquerors.webp]]

## Effects
Map Bosses have 100% increased chance to drop a Conqueror Map
(Conquerors are Baran, the Crusader, Veritania, the Redeemer, Al-Hezmin, the Hunter and Drox, the Warlord)
(Final Map Boss in Tier 14+ Maps have a 2% base chance to drop a Conqueror Map)
