# Effects
Capturable Beasts in your Maps have Bestial Rage
Defeating capturable Beasts grants a stack of Hunters' Cunning
(Hunters' Cunning grants less damage taken from Beasts, increased damage against Beasts, and increased Quantity and Rarity of items found from slain Beasts)
(Bestial Rage increases the Movement Speed, Attack Speed and Damage of the affected Beast)
