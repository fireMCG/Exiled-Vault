# Legion General Chance

![[Exiled Vault Keystones/_Images/PoE1 - Legion General Chance.webp]]

## Effects
Legion Encounters in your Maps are 20% more likely to include a General
(Legion Generals are Queen Hyrri Ngamaku, General Marceus Lioneye, Cardinal Sanctus Vox, Viper Napuatzi and Aukuna, the Black Sekhema)
