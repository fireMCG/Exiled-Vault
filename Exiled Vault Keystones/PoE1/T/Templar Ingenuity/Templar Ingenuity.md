# Templar Ingenuity

![[Exiled Vault Keystones/_Images/PoE1 - Templar Ingenuity.webp]]

## Effects
Blight Towers in your Maps can be Salvaged after the Blight Encounter
Higher Tier Towers grant better Salvaged Rewards
Salvaged Rewards are improved for each different Tower Type built during Encounter
