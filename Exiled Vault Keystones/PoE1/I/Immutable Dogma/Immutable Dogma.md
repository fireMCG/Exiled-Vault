# Immutable Dogma

![[Exiled Vault Keystones/_Images/PoE1 - Immutable Dogma.webp]]

## Description
Do not doubt. Do not question.
This is the way it has always been.
This is the way it shall always be.

## Effects
2% increased Maps found in your Maps
Cannot Reroll Favours at Ritual Altars in your Maps
Monsters Sacrificed at Ritual Altars in your Maps grant (100—150)% more Tribute
Unmodifiable
