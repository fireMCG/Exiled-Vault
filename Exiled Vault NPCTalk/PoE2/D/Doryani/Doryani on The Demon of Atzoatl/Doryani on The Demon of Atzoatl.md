I now know her to be... Alva Valai. We did determine that the pair were coming from the future, but we could never have guessed how far. It's still unclear when those events are meant to happen... for her. Also... {why?} Why will she go back and aid a rampage through Atzoatl? Actually, maybe that one's obvious.
