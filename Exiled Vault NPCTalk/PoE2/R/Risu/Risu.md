#### Risu on Clearing the Way
Risu: The other {akharas} must join the Gifting of Water if Asala is to become {Sekhema} of {Sekhemas}. The land is still plagued by the passing of the Beast. They cannot get through. May I ask you, my friend, to journey out to the other end of the Khari Crossing? The way is plagued by a great scorpion and a massive sandworm, both crazed by Corruption. Slay them, so that the others can reach us.
