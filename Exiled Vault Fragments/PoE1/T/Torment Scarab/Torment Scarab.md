![[Exiled Vault Fragments/_Images/PoE1 - Torment Scarab.webp]]

## Description
Their crimes in life haunt you in death.

## Details
Map Fragments
Stack Size: 1 / 20
Limit: 2
Area is haunted by 4 additional Tormented Spirits

Tormented Spirits in Area have a 10% chance to

be set free when Possessed Monsters are slain

and dig up a Possessed Monster
Can be used in a personal Map Device to add modifiers to a Map.
Shift click to unstack.
