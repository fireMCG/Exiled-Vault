## Description

## Details
Map Fragments
Stack Size: 1 / 20
Limit: 3
Rare Monsters in Area have a quarter chance to be Possessed by up to 3 Tormented Spirits
Can be used in a personal Map Device to add modifiers to a Map.
Shift click to unstack.
