## The Winter Sekhema
Do they still call me the Winter Sekhema? I am always surprised at the lasting memory of my people. We survived the Winter of the World so long ago that the Vastiri has endured two or three entire eras since. There was a time of warming, of rains, of lush rivers and forests... then the slow growth of the sands and the salt flats... the passing of my beloved mastodons... and now the deserts, too, begin to change. This is my wisdom for you: if you wait long enough, all things change.
