## Overextend
Support
Attack , Critical
Tier: 2
Level: 1
Cost Multiplier: 115%
Support Requirements : +5 Dex
Supports Attack Skills you use yourself. Supported Skills deal more Damage with Critical Hits , but Critically Hitting an Enemy with them will Daze you for a short time.
You are Dazed for 5 seconds on Critically Striking with Supported Skill
Supported Skills have 30 % more Critical Damage Bonus
