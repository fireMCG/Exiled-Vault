## Volt
Support
Attack , Projectile , Lightning , Chaining
Tier: 2
Level: 1
Cost Multiplier: 120%
Support Requirements : +5 Dex
Supports Projectile Attack Skills you use yourself. Supported Skills gain Voltaic Charge as you move, up to a maximum. When used, Supported Skills expend that Charge to gain extra Lightning Damage and Chain .
Supported Skills gain 3 Voltaic Charge per Metre travelled
Supported Skills have 30 maximum Voltaic Charge Supported Skills consume all Voltaic Charge on use
Supported Skills Chain 1 time per ten Voltaic Charge consumed
Supported Skills gain 5 % of Damage as extra Lightning Damage per ten Voltaic Charge consumed
