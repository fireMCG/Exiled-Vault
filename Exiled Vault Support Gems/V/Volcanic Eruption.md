## Volcanic Eruption
Support
Attack , AoE , Melee , Strike , Projectile , Trigger , Fire
Tier: 3
Level: 1
Cost Multiplier: 120%
Support Requirements : +5 Str
Supports Melee Strike Skills you use yourself. Supported Skills trigger Volcanic Eruption on Hit against Ignited Enemies.
Supported Strikes trigger Volcanic Eruption on Hitting Ignited Enemies
Cooldown Time: 0.40 s
Attack Damage: 40% of base
Fire molten Projectiles from the struck target, which will land nearby dealing Attack damage in an area.
Converts 80 % of Physical Damage to Fire Damage
Impact radius is 1 metre
Fires 4 Molten Projectiles
