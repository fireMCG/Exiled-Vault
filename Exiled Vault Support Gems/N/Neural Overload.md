## Neural Overload
Support
Lightning
Tier: 3
Level: 1
Support Requirements : +5 Dex
Supports any skill that Hits enemies. Nearby enemies are marked when they are Primed for Electrocution , and hitting a marked enemy with a supported skill will Electrocute them.
