# Flavour Text
The Liege of the Lightless seeks dominion for the authority it brings.

# Details
- Limited to: 1 Ancient Augment
- Requires: Level 60

# Effects
- Helmets: Remove a Damaging Ailment when you use a Command Skill
- Body Armours: +2 to Armour per 1 Spirit
- Boots: 1% increased Movement Speed per 15 Spirit, up to a maximum of 40% / Other Modifiers to Movement Speed except for Sprinting do not apply
