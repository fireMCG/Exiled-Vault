# Commander Kirac Zana's Plan

## Lore
Zana intends to destroy the Atlas? It all makes sense now. The Atlas has caused her nothing but pain her entire life. I can't even begin to recount what she lost - her father, her childhood, her friends, the man she loved... I'm not sure what the consequences will be if she actually manages to bring the Atlas down... but I understand.
