# Commander Kirac The Originator

## Lore
So who is the 'Originator,' after all? Is it Zana? Working this job, I hear wild new things every day, but it'll take me some time to make sense of this. I like to think we were decently close, Zana and I, and she never mentioned a brother... or her mother, come to think of it... but Eagon has proven he truly does care for her, at the very least. If you get the chance, try to convince Zana to return to us. We could sorely use her aid.
