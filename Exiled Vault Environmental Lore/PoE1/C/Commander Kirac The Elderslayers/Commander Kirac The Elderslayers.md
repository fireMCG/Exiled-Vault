# Commander Kirac The Elderslayers

## Lore
They were not the first to find the Atlas, but they may have been the most impactful. They worked to seal away an entity called The Elder that dominated that realm... and that victory cost most of them their sanity. Not long after, I worked with Zana and a powerful exile to defeat the mad Elderslayers, who by then fancied themselves Conquerors. Unfortunately, we were too slow, and the leader of the Elderslayers, Sirus, escaped from the Atlas and devastated Oriath's rebuilding efforts. Talk about kicking us while we're down...
