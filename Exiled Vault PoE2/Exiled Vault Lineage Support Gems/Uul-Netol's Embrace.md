# Uul-Netol's Embrace
"The Lords could not breathe, so they grew new lungs.  
The Lords could not venture, so they grew new skin.  
The Lords were alone, so they grew us, to serve them."

## Effects
Break Armour equal to 20% of Chaos Damage dealt by Supported Skills
Supported Skills Gain 20% of Physical Damage as Extra Chaos Damage
