# Garukhan's Resolve
At the last, her hope gone - but not her resolve - she threw her  
beloved Tangletongue. That was the day a god bled. For this, the  
Great Roc graced a Maraketh warrior with a feather for the first time.

#
## Effects
Attack Critical Hit Chance with Supported Skills Bifurcates
Supported Skills become usable after you have moved 20 metres
Supported Skills become unusable again after use
