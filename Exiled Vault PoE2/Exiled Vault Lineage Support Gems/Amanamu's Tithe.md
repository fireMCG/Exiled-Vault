# Amanamu's Tithe
Those that serve the Liege of the Lightless are his, utterly.  
Blood, flesh, and soul are fodder to bend to his will.  
Under a Lich Lord, not even death is an escape.

#
## Effects
50% chance to gain an Abyssal Monster Modifier when a Minion from Supported Skills dies
Maximum 3 Abyssal Monster Modifiers gained this way
